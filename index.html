Intro

Welcome to my Frontend Masters Blog, a page detailing my experiences using Frontend Masters, and comparing it to platforms such as Scrimba and Codecademy.

First Impressions

Simon Waller

November 07

My first impressions of the Frontend Masters website is that it has been well designed, and appears very clean.

It is also easy to navigate, and it doesn't take forever to find what you're looking for.

FM vs Codecademy

Simon Waller

November 07

Codecademy allows you to program inside a terminal, whereas Frontend Masters is typically using a more traditional video tutorial layout.

This may be a negative to some people, but I will explain further later why I prefer this setup.

FM vs Scrimba

Simon Waller

November 07

Scrimba also offers a terminal where you can code directly inside the tutorials. This is vastly superior to Codecademy, and is updated more regularly.

Again we'll discuss further why there are pros and cons to Scrimba over Frontend Masters at a later date.
